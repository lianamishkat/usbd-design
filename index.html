potential for american companies

According to The Asian Development Bank projection for 2019, Bangladesh will attain the highest GDP growth in Southeast Asia with a rate of 8%, followed by India at 7.2%. Additionally, the World Bank has included Bangladesh in its ‘Middle-income Countries’ (MICs) list in 2018 due to its per capita income growth in recent years.

Consumer spending in Bangladesh has tripled in the last ten years, resulting in potential opportunities for U.S. companies to explore the market for products and services. Major oil and gas companies and energy giants such as GE have also been actively pursuing business interests in Bangladesh.

Due to inexpensive labor costs and increasing consumer spending, Bangladesh offers a very positive prospect for U.S. investors. With our vast knowledge of the country, we will be able to provide top-notch support for your business to thrive.

a 'hotspot' for apparel sourcing

Bangladesh is the third largest supplier of apparel in the U.S. and its competitive edge as a supplier is growing due to low labor costs and improvement in productivity, infrastructure, and government incentives for exporters. With development in China’s economy as well, Bangladesh has become a more attractive source for apparel products.

According to McKinsey Institute, Bangladesh will soon become one of the hotspots for apparel product sourcing; Of the 28 major apparel buyers in the U.S. and Europe, 89% stated that they consider Bangladesh among the top three sourcing destinations.

USBD Market Access is appropriately positioned to establish an effective and efficient link between buyers in the U.S. and the “makers” in Bangladesh.
